Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to commission a pair?

The timeline varies based on complexity and current workload. Typically, bespoke orders take between 3-6 weeks from initial consultation to final delivery. Rush orders may be accommodated with an additional fee.

Do I need to visit your workshop in person?

While an in-person visit is ideal for precise measurements and material selection, we do accommodate remote commissions. We can guide you through self-measurement and provide material samples shipped to your location.

What is your pricing range?

Our bespoke footwear starts at ₦25,000 for simple designs and can go up to ₦150,000 or more for complex designs with exotic materials. The final price depends on materials, complexity, and detailing required.

Can I provide my own design?

Absolutely! We welcome custom designs and can work with your sketches or inspiration images to create exactly what you envision. Our artisans will consult with you on feasibility and recommend adjustments if needed.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship worldwide. Shipping costs and timelines vary by destination. Typically, international deliveries take 1-3 weeks after completion, depending on the destination and customs processing.

Our Heritage

Inseekan Leather was born from a passion for preserving traditional Nigerian craftsmanship while meeting the demands of modern style. Our journey began in 2015 when our founder, recognizing a gap in the market for high-quality, locally-made leather footwear, decided to create a brand that would showcase the best of Nigerian artistry.

Starting as a small workshop with just two artisans, we've grown into a respected name in bespoke footwear, serving clients across Nigeria and internationally. Our name "Inseekan" draws from the Yoruba language, meaning "to find" or "to discover" - representing our mission to help clients discover the perfect footwear that expresses their individuality.

Every pair we create tells a story - of skilled hands that cut and stitch, of premium materials carefully selected, and of a client's unique personality expressed through design. We honor the time-honored techniques passed down through generations while innovating to meet contemporary tastes.

Our Craftsmanship

At Inseekan Leather, we follow a meticulous process that honors traditional techniques while ensuring modern precision:

  • Material Selection: We source premium leather from both local Nigerian tanneries and select international suppliers
  • Pattern Making: Each design begins with precise patterns tailored to the client's measurements
  • Hand Cutting: Artisans carefully cut each component using specialized tools and techniques
  • Traditional Stitching: We use both machine and hand-stitching methods for durability and aesthetic appeal
  • Finishing: Each pair undergoes meticulous finishing, including polishing, edge treatment, and quality inspection

This careful attention to detail ensures that every pair of Inseekan footwear not only looks exceptional but provides comfort and durability that lasts for years.

We are Known For

Bespoke Experience

Every pair begins with a personal consultation. Your shoe is designed uniquely for you—choose style, material, color, and details. We take precise measurements for a flawless fit.

Custom Last & Finest Leather

For true bespoke, a custom "last" (wooden foot form) may be crafted to your precise foot shape. Only the best full-grain and top-grain leathers are used, locally sourced and imported from Italy.

Handcrafted to Last

Master artisans employ traditional techniques—like Goodyear welting and Blake stitching—for shoes that can be resoled and enjoyed for decades. No glue, no shortcuts: just pure craftsmanship.

Signature Styles

From timeless Oxfords and Derbies to bespoke loafers, sandals, boots, and statement pieces—each design is tailored for your lifestyle and taste.
